About the Codex

The Gielinor Codex is a personal, ever-growing collection of Old School RuneScape guides — built for clarity, depth, and aesthetics that match the world they describe.

Every guide is written to be both a quick-reference for veterans and a friendly starting point for new accounts. No paywalls, no fluff, no bloat — just the information you actually need to play efficiently.

New guides are added regularly. Check back often.
